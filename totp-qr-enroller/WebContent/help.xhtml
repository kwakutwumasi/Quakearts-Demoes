Using Microsoft Authenticator App:
  1. Use the links above to download the app onto your phone

  2. When the app has completed installation, open it. Click on "Add Account" to begin

  3. Click on "Other Account (Google, Facebook, etc.)"

  4. Scan the QR Code

  5. Your user account and name will appear under the "fastpacetransfer" name. Click on it

  6. Enter the code shown under the "One-time password code" label into the OTP Code Text box, then click "Activate". If you get an error message contact the System Administrator

Using Google Authenticator App:
  1. Use the links above to download the app onto your phone

  2. When the app has completed installation, open it. Click on "+" sign on the bottom right corner to begin

  3. Click on "Scan QR Code"

  4. Scan the QR Code

  5. Enter the code shown under the "fastpacetransfer.com" label into the OTP Code Text box, then click "Activate". If you get an error message contact the System Administrator
